1.DIY FAN USING USB CABLE..

You will need;

  1. OLD USB CABLE
  2. DC MOTOR (5V)
  3. WIRES
  4. PLASTIC SHEET(TO MAKE THE PROPELLERS)
  5. A WOODEN STICK (OR ANYTHING SIMILAR)

HOW TO MAKE IT?

FIRST WE TAKE THE POSITIVE WIRE OF THE USB CABLE AND ATTACH IT TO THE POSITIVE OF THE DC MOTOR. THEN DO THE SAME WITH THE NEGATIVE WIRE,ATTACH IT TO THE NEGATIVE OF THE DC MOTOR .THEN PASTE THE MOTOR ON ONE SIDE OF THE STICK AND THE USB END TO THE OTHER SIDE OF THE STICK AS SHOWN IN THE VIDEO .

2.SIMPLE DIY POWERBANK IN 60 SECONDS.

You will need;

  1. POWERBANK MODULE

OUR FIRST STEP IS TO CONNECT WIRES TO THE BATTERIES IN PARALEL AS SHOWN IN THE ABOVE VIDEO.THEN WE CONNECT THE POSITVE AND NEGATIVES WIRES TO THE POSITIVE AND NEGATIVE TERMINAL OF THE POWER BANK MODULE RESPECTFULLY .WRAP IT UP WITH ANY DESIRED MATERIAL.THATS ALL IT TAKES TO BUILD THIS.
